April Memories 💗

This is one of my favorite pictures of us because I was the one who took it, making it even more special to me. It was also the day we went out and had our very first Sunday date together. I was so happy because we finally got to spend the morning together, creating memories and enjoying each other’s company. Even though the weather was hot, we endured it without complaint because what mattered most was being together and having the chance to go out and share those precious moments. Looking back at this picture, it reminds me of how happy and grateful I was to be by your side, making simple moments feel so meaningful and unforgettable.

This picture was taken during our very first video call while you were at work. It holds a special place in my heart because that call became one of the greatest sources of reassurance in our relationship. On that day, I felt my heart becoming lighter and more at peace because being with you never felt like a burden or something that brought pressure it was simply us, laughing, teasing each other, and enjoying every moment together. This is the first relationship I have ever been in where, as time passes, I feel more secure instead of more anxious. You never let me overthink for days or weeks because you always take the time to understand me and give me the reassurance I need. That is why I promise to give all that love back to you and even more. I will cherish your love, stay loyal to you, and do my best to match the kindness, patience, and love that you continuously give to me.

This was the day you chose a perfume for me. Even though it took us a while to decide, you patiently put in the effort to find the scent that suited me best. It was the first time someone had ever gone out of their way to choose a perfume especially for me, and that meant more than you know. I still remember the way your face lit up every time you smelled the fragrance you picked, and I don't think I'll ever forget either its scent or its name. Whenever I have the chance to buy a perfume from that same store again, I will choose the one you loved for me, because it was you who carefully picked it with so much thought and affection. What you gave me that day was more than just a perfume you gave me your time, your effort, and the warmth of simply being there beside me, and that is the gift I will always treasure.

This was the time we ate street food at the plaza. It wasn’t actually our first time, because we had already shared a night market meal on our first meet, and it eventually became one of our little dating spots. That moment made me realize that we don’t need fancy restaurants or fast food to be happy together street food is more than enough for us. As we ate, we talked about our future plans, our dreams for ourselves, for our relationship, and for the life we hope to build ahead. In that simple place, with simple food, everything felt complete because it was filled with you.

The last picture we have from April was during our call while I was at the clinic. That day, I was about to get my braces, and I was honestly so scared of going to places like that, especially when it comes to facing doctors. While I was waiting, you decided to call me, and it helped me so much even though you were at work. My fear and nervousness just disappeared the moment I saw you and heard your voice. I also won’t forget that day when you saw the result of my braces your reaction was so cute, and I’ll always remember your smiles and how genuinely happy and proud you were of me for improving and changing for the better.
